# Project Overview

LibroSchool is a web-first platform for Italian high school students.

Main features:
- used school book selling
- book requests
- school-based discovery
- classroom rooms
- notes
- flashcards
- messaging
- reports

The MVP must stay simple.

Do not add:
- payment
- shipping
- mobile app
- parent portal
- official school management features
- grades
- attendance
- official signatures
- AI-heavy functionality
- complex social networking

Business principle:
Books bring users in.
Notes, classrooms, and flashcards keep users active.
